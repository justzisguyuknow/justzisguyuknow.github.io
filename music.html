Music for Good Times

I'm a singer and guitarist based in Berwyn, PA. I play an eclectic mix of rock, folk, and Brazilian music covers. I play both acoustic and electric guitar, solo or with bass/percussion accompaniment. I'm also in Not Your Mama's Drama, a Berwyn-based rock & pop cover band.

I am available for gigs around the Philly Upper Main Line, Phoenixville, and surrounding areas.
